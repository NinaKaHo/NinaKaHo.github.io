Our Company and Community

History and Background


Badoom was founded 2024.

It all started in a small home with a thought of sharing old methods that are pure and safe to consume and which will help many to feel better from inside out. We are not only a skincare specialists but our passion is to give you also wellness through nutrition and apply our greatest knowledge to your daily routines. Nowadays there are plenty of cosmetics and foods that are not necessarily good or even safe for humans to use. Plenty or microplastics and carsinogens around.



Company Mission and Values


We strive on knowledge and our community is based on feedback and research. Our mission is to find every customer the right products and be with them on their journey to wellbeing day to day life.

We truly value each customer, and that is why we call you dear custumers part of our community. We do not just sell products or services, but also experiences and knowledge about yourself. What works for you, works for us as a company. We do guarantee you having correct products for you consumption and if you are not satisfied, we will refund you this experience in full.



Ethics and our Special Team


Badoom is a cruelty free brand that does not test any products or ingredients on animals.

We are aiming to recycle as much as possible and this is considered in the community instructions of the products received. On top of recycling we are non-waste company. This meaning that all waste will go to purposefull usage and benefit of the next purpose. As an example we do use compost and produce our own soil from all the natural cosmetics waste.

We also reccomend you to do the same or provide us your products for a nice discount!
