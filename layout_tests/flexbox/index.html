Cakes

Cakes are baked desserts that come in a variety of flavors, textures, and shapes. They are typically made from a combination of flour, sugar, eggs, butter or oil, and flavorings such as vanilla, chocolate, or fruits.

Ice Cream and Frozen Desserts

Ice cream is a frozen dessert made from dairy products such as milk and cream, sweeteners, and flavorings. It is churned while freezing to incorporate air and create a smooth, creamy texture.

Pastries

Pastries encompass a wide range of baked goods that are made from dough, often layered, and can be sweet or savory. Sweet pastries are commonly enjoyed as desserts.
